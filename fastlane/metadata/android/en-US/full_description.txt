<p>BrownPaper is an offline-first read-later app for Android with optional wallabag sync. Save articles from the share sheet, keep readable text and hero images on your device, and read them in a focused native reader whenever you have time.</p><p>Features:</p><ul><li>Save links directly from Android sharing</li><li>Sync articles, tags, favorites, archive state, and annotations with a wallabag account</li><li>Read extracted article text offline with adjustable fonts, themes, and content width</li><li>Highlight passages and add notes while keeping annotations available offline</li><li>Search and organize articles with tags, folders, favorites, read state, and archive state</li><li>Backup and restore local app data</li></ul><p>No account is required. wallabag sync is optional, and saved articles remain available on the device.</p>
